linear_mixed_effects: Linear mixed effects modeling
===================================================

Linear mixed effects models evaluate the contribution of exogenous covariates "group_categories" to a single dependent variable, "metric". Perform LME and plot line plots of each group column. A feature table artifact is required input, though whether "metric" is derived from the feature table or metadata is optional.
